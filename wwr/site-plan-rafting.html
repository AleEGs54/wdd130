Overview

Purpose

To promote the rafting trips we do, show people what we offer to them, including: Planned trips, Packages, Events, Destinations, and much more! This webpage is the virtual face of what we do and how we do it. It's purpose is to bring people to know who we are in the best way possible to build a revenue.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on): Are groups of families or friends, usually going on vacations. They ussualy have a good budget, but also want to save money. What do they want? They are looking for a space where they can experience new emotions, feel apart from the world and to have space for themselves. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They will find featured destinations, packages, how to contact us, what they can expect from a trip, adaptations to different budgets, etc. What are their needs that aren't being met? Places where to relax and disconnect from the world. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Through desktop, laptop and tablets.
